About us

The DIU CIS Club (Computing and Information Systems Club) at Daffodil International University is a student organization with the motto "Code. Lead. Inspire," dedicated to enhancing technical skills, promoting leadership, and fostering a collaborative community among students.

Programming Wing: This wing focuses on improving students' coding skills through workshops, contests, and collaborative learning sessions.

Cultural Wing: This wing celebrates diversity and creativity by organizing cultural events, performances, and festivals.

Sports Wing: This wing promotes sportsmanship and teamwork through indoor and outdoor sports tournaments, including the "CIS Premier League".

What's the mission of our Club

Foster a Community of Innovators: The club aims to create a collaborative environment where students passionate about programming, leadership, and technology can connect.

Enhance Technical Skills: Promoting "programming excellence" through workshops, contests, and learning opportunities.

Promote Leadership Development: Encouraging students to embrace leadership roles in club activities.

Bridge Academia and Industry: Organizing industry visits and networking events to help students align academic skills with real-world industry demands.
