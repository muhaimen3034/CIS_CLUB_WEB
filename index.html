Experience Global Connections of Code and AI

Refers to the Computing and Information System (CIS) Club at Daffodil International University (DIU) in Bangladesh. The club's motto is "Code. Lead. Inspire.", and it is an active student organization within the university.

About Us

The DIU CIS Club focuses on fostering a community for students interested in programming, leadership, and collaboration related to the field of Computing and Information Systems. It is one of many student clubs at DIU, which also has a dedicated Department of CIS.

Who we are

The CIS Club (Computing and Information System Club) are students and faculty members associated with the Department of Computing and Information System (CIS) at Daffodil International University (DIU) in Dhaka, Bangladesh

which primarily focus on the university, the department's history (commenced after 2002), or other long-standing clubs like the DIU Computer and Programming Club (established in 2011).
